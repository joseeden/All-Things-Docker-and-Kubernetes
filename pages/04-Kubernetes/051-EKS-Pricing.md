
### Amazon EKS - Pricing

What you pay for:

- EKS cluster per hour
- resources related to running your apps 

What you don't pay for:

- the control plane

To learn more, check out the official [Amazon EKS Pricing](https://aws.amazon.com/eks/pricing/) page.
